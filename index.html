About

I am Peizheng Wang (王沛峥), currently a PhD student at Imperial College London.

My research focuses on machine learning methods for physiological signals and wearable sensors, with applications in fatigue, sleep disorders, cognitive impairment, MCI, and Alzheimer's disease.

More broadly, I aim to use AI technologies to better understand physiological processes and improve health, safety, and well-being.
