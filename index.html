About

I am Peizheng Wang (王沛峥), currently a PhD student at Imperial College London. My work focuses on applying artificial intelligence to better understand physiological signals and human health-related behaviors.

I am particularly interested in AI-driven approaches for fatigue monitoring, sleep disorder assessment, cognitive impairment analysis, and early risk identification for neurodegenerative conditions such as MCI and Alzheimer's disease, with the goal of improving health, safety, and overall well-being.
